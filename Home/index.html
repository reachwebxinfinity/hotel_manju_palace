Our Neighbourhood

The Nine Streets, or De Negen Straatjes as they’re called in Dutch, are home to some of the city’s hidden gems. Homegrown brands, speciality cafes and gourmet restaurants line the streets, making the canal-lined areas one of the most happening places to hangout in the Dam. Eat well and shop happy.

Fou Fow Ramen

One of the first Japanese ramen restaurants in Amsterdam and still a locals favourite. Expect a queue outside but it's def. worth the wait. Put your name on the list and they'll call you whenever a table is ready. Pair your ramen order with a chilled Japanese beer and you're all set. Check them out

Anna+Nina

The to go to shop for unique collections of silver & gold jewellery, high quality fashion and the latest trends in homeware. Founded by two Dutch female entrepreneurs who get their inspiration from travelling to exotic places. Shop now

Toki

Just outside of the Nine Streets you can find TOKI, one of the best coffee spots in town for fine coffee & loose leaf tea. Go eat

De Nieuwe Kerk

De Nieuwe Kerk, right on Damsquare, offers high-profile exhibitions about art, photography, and inspiring individuals and cultures. Every year it’s the world premiere location for the ‘World Press Photo Exhibition’ which showcases stories that matter. Explore

Grachtenhuismuseum

A canal museum? It’s more interesting than it sounds, we promise. A series of projections, holograms and large-scale models guide you round the famous old inner city of Amsterdam, showing both its historical background and its present-day significance. Explore

Restaurant Felix

Just a five-minute canal stroll from The Hox you can find Felix Meritis, an imposing canal house which resides Restaurant Felix. Head Chef Floris takes you on a fine dining experience with his so-called New Dutch Cuisine which is creative and will make your taste buds explode. Ask Sommelier Saskia about the wine-pairing. Take me there

Canal Houses

The Hoxton is situated in the middle of the canal district. Doesn’t matter if you turn left of right, canal houses, bridges with bikes, picturesque alley’s and royal palaces or churches are just a few steps away.

Marie-Stella-Maris

This is what we call a WIN-WIN situation. Grab the highest quality natural care products and get to support clean water projects worldwide at the same time. Stop by at their shop, which is a feast for the eye, to try, test and learn more about their products which are renowned for their fragrances. Buy Good, Do Good

Van der Linde

This generations-old ice-cream shop only offers Vanilla Ice Cream (yep, that's it!). Don't expect experimental flavours & stacks of scoops and toppings. Their secret recipe of Vanilla Ice Cream is sooo tasty and creamy (it’s almost like Whipped Cream) that you really don't need anything else.
